Smart Agriculture with Blockchain and AI

Drone Capturing Land Details

Drones are used to capture high-quality land details, including:

  • Longitude/Latitude
  • High-quality images
  • Environmental data

Data Sent Securely to Private Blockchain

The drone's data is sent securely to a private blockchain. Different nodes process the data using AI/ML algorithms, and the results are used for:

  • Farmer payments
  • Marketplace management
  • Providing tips to improve crops

AI/ML Algorithms Used

  • CNN: Identifies crop diseases
  • YOLO: Analyzes crop conditions
  • LSTM: Predicts weather patterns and their impacts on crops

Automated Payments with Smart Contracts

Smart contracts ensure automated payments to eligible farmers without intermediaries. Payments are made based on the data stored in the blockchain marketplace.

Tips to Improve Crops

Farmers receive analyzed data that helps improve their crops, including weather patterns, crop diseases, and market demand insights.
